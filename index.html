Payment Audit · OnePipe

Cortex: Solving the Ghost Debit Panic

A 3-phase embedded finance case study. Phase 1 identified three critical friction points in the OnePipe merchant payment flow — Ghost Debit Panic, Jargon Paralysis, and Confirmation Abandonment — each backed by behavioural signals including Rage Clicks, Dead Clicks, and Long Time on Page.

Phase 2 applied ICE scoring and a Priority Matrix, producing a UX Friction Report, executive brief, and 3-slide stakeholder summary. Phase 3 (Cortex Challenge) built a financial operating system from a blank canvas — defining problem space, target user, solution architecture, and North Star metrics without existing data.

Post-launch architecture review by Apple Quality Program Manager — 7 governance points
₦8M daily transaction volume recoverable
3 friction points identified & prioritised
Growth Strategy · BeReal

Scaling Retention via User-Controlled Circles

Uncovered the Ghost Town Effect — declining daily engagement despite high brand affinity. Led focus groups that reframed the core product insight: privacy is not about hiding, it is about control. This drove a full feature pivot from a solo streak to a selective Streak Circle model.

Iterated across two prototype versions based on usability data. Final feature achieved 100% brand alignment. Endorsed by the CEO of Extern for analytical rigour and data-driven adaptability.

80% projected engagement lift
100% brand alignment achieved
KYC Audit · Bamboo

Optimising Onboarding: Fixing False Progress

Audited the Bamboo KYC/AML onboarding flow to identify a pattern of False Progress — users believing they had completed onboarding when they had not, causing silent drop-off with no re-entry mechanism.

Created product logic tables mapping regulatory banking requirements against a frictionless user onboarding experience, and recommended a targeted re-entry flow to recover lost users at the point of failure.

1 critical silent drop-off pattern fixed
KYC re-entry flow designed
